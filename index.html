The Flickering Spire

This adventure is designed to entertain you and your friends for hours with heroic deeds and harrowing perils. You, the Game Master (GM), will guide your friends as they explore the ancient Sablewood, banter with its strange denizens, and defeat vicious monsters with sword, spell, and Valor.

The Setting

The Sablewood is an ancient, expansive forest, older than memory, where the canopy is so thick that day and night are often indistinguishable. It is a land of vibrant life, home to unique hybrid fauna and reclusive communities. To maintain a fragile peace, great Spires of stone were erected, tended by solitary Keepers whose life force is tied to the magical flames they protect. These beacons ward the forest against a creeping, mindless blight and pacify the more monstrous creatures that lurk in the shadows.

The Threat

A subtle corruption has begun to seep into the Sablewood. It is a formless, insidious blight that preys on weakness and amplifies despair. It twists the natural life of the forest, turning creatures into pained, aggressive versions of themselves. This blight originates from a source of chaotic, uncontrolled magic, and it spreads through contact and proximity, threatening to unravel the ancient wards that have long protected the forest.

Recent Developments

A powerful surge of raw, untamed magic recently washed through the Sablewood. The source was unknown to Elara, the keeper of the western Spire. Fearing the surge would overwhelm her Spire's wards, she attempted a dangerous attunement ritual. The power was too great. The ritual backfired, shattering her defenses and allowing the latent corruption to pour into her. She is no longer the Spire's guardian but its prisoner, a vessel for the very blight she sought to defy.

Adventure Summary

The adventure begins when the Whitefire Arcanist, concerned about a weakening Spire, tasks the PCs with investigating its failing flame. Their journey will take them along the Sablewood Path where they will encounter signs of the spreading corruption. They will discover the blighted hamlet of Ashen and learn of the Spirekeeper's fate before navigating the mystical Miremist to reach the Spire itself. After ascending the crumbling tower, they will face the corrupted Spirekeeper, Elara, with the choice to either cleanse her and the Spire or destroy the vessel she has become to stop the blight's spread.

Adventure Hooks

1. The Underroot Connection

You grew up in an Underroot community and have a deep connection to the Sablewood's health. You've felt a "sickness" in the roots and heard whispers from your kin that the surface world is falling out of balance. The dimming of a Spire is a dire omen you feel compelled to investigate.

2. A Merchant's Debt

You are a member of, or have a significant debt to, the Sable Sinecure merchant's guild. The increasing aggression of the forest's hybrid fauna is making the well-worn pathways unsafe, disrupting trade. The guild has put out a bounty for anyone who can discover the source of the recent animal agitation and "pacify" it.

3. Spire-Sworn Lineage

One of your ancestors was a Spirekeeper. Though you never knew them, you carry a small, inert warding totem. For the past week, it has begun to feel warm and pulse with a faint, sickly light. You feel a pull towards the west, an instinctual call to answer a distress you don't yet understand.

Concluding the Adventure

With Elara either cleansed or laid to rest, the immediate threat to the western Sablewood has passed.

  • If Elara was Cleansed: The Spire's flame roars back to life, a brilliant, steady beacon. The corruption in the immediate area recedes over the next few days. The villagers of Ashen slowly recover, and the forest's fauna returns to a less aggressive state. Elara, once she recovers, is immensely grateful and becomes a powerful ally to the party. She will confirm that the surge of magic felt overwhelming and unnatural, providing a hook for a future adventure to find its source.
  • If Elara was Destroyed: The flame is extinguished completely, and the Spire goes dark. The blighted ground around its base remains, a permanent scar on the landscape. While the party has stopped the immediate threat, the region is now vulnerable, and the corruption may one day return. The party has the gratitude of the people of Ashen, but a shadow of failure and loss hangs over their victory. This could lead to a future quest to find a new Spirekeeper or a way to relight the flame.

In either case, the party has earned a reputation as heroes in the Sablewood, and the Whitefire Arcanist will be keen to hear of their deeds and perhaps offer them a new, more dangerous task.
